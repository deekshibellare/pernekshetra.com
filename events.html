Events Timeline

Events that are going on in our temple right now.

  • Puttari(Huttari)

    Wednesday 15 October 2015

    Huttari festival was observed with grandeur and traditional fervour in Perne. Harvest festival was celbated with joy and new rice food was served to all the gatherings. Service of Perne Muchilot Bhagavathi Komara Late Shree Shankara Komara was rembered on the occasion.

  • Medical Camp

    Date to be updated

    Medical camp to address the health care needs of community was conducted at Perne. The purpose of organizing the camp was to make the people aware of their health. A team of well-known eye specialist, physicians, dentist general practice doctors attended the camp. The doctors examined each patient one by one, diagnosing all weaknesses and illnesses. They checked the eyes, lungs, functioning of heart and other parts of body and prescribes medicine and all the medicines given free of charge. Patients with eye problems were given free medical care, advice and spectacles.
